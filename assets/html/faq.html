1. WHAT IS AMANA INTERNET BANKING?

Amana Internet Banking Facility offers you financial services via the Internet. It also offers value-added services you do not normally enjoy at the banking counters in the most customer friendly manner.

To perform any transactions in the Amana Internet Banking Facility, you would need to have an Amana Internet Banking User ID, Password and PIN.

2. WHAT SERVICES DOES AMANA INTERNET BANKING FACILITY OFFER?

         Online access to any of your accounts, Current/Savings/Term Deposits/Assets & Liabilities

         Balance Inquiry

         Account Statements View/Request for Hard Copy or export to Excel, PDF or direct print.

         Funds Transfer to your own Accounts

         Recurring Transfers (Standing Order Transactions)

         Define Favorite Beneficiaries

         Fund Transfers to any Account with-in Amana Bank

         View Personal Information

 

3. WHO IS ELIGIBLE TO OBTAIN AMANA INTERNET BANKING FACILITY?

Any person maintaining an account at any of the Amana Bank branches.

 

4. IS INTERNET BANKING SECURE AND SAFE?

Amana Internet Banking Facility uses Dual Authentication that is that you're given a Login Password and a Transaction PIN for transactions. Data transmitted between your PC and & Amana Internet Banking site are strongly encrypted, so that no intruder can read what is being transmitted. Above all, you have your own user ID, Password and Transaction PIN for extra security. You also have the facility to change your password and transaction PIN anytime you wish.

6. HOW TO MAKE THE AMANA INTERNET BANKING USAGE MORE SECURE.

You play a major role in maintaining the security of your Amana Internet Banking operations.

Here are a few pointers:

         Memorize your User ID, Password and PIN and never write them down anywhere nor reveal them to anyone. Your User ID and Password will authenticate you when you log on to System.

         Change your Password/PIN from time to time, using the "My Profile" --> "Change Password" / Change PIN feature on the Amana Internet Banking System.

         Before you exit from the System, always clear your browser's disk cache/ temporary files to prevent someone from being able to access the information on your computer.

         Before leaving the PC please close the Browser.

         Notify the Bank immediately if you notice any unusual account activity.

GETTING STARTED

10. WHEN CAN I START USING THE AMANA INTERENT BANKING?

On receipt of duly completed and signed Amana Internet Banking application form from you, the Bank will scrutinize the application and create your User ID in the System and then the User ID will be sent to the E-mail ID you have provided in the application form.

Then the Password and the Transaction PIN will be posted to your registered correspondence address.

Once you receive your Password & PIN, you are requested to Call 0117756756 and activate the Internet Banking Account.

During the first login, it is mandatory to change both, the Password and Transaction PIN and an additional security feature is where you have to provide a Security question and an answer and a Passphrase(Eg: I Like Amana Bank) This requirement is implemented for your own security.

 

11. DO I NEED TO INSTALL ANYTHING BEFORE I CAN USE AMANA INTERNET BANKING FACILITY?

You should have the SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS as specified above. Nothing else is required.

12. HOW DO I KNOW WHETHER THE TRANSACTIONS GOT SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED?

Once you submit the transaction a message will appear if the transaction is successful Your request has been accepted and will be processed shortly. Thank you for using Amana online services.

You can also view your last transaction in the Activity History Log for further clarification.

14. IF I ENCOUNTER DIFFICULTIES WITH TRANSACTIONS ON AMANA INTERNET BANKING FACILITY?

You may call the Help Desk at Tel: +94 11 7756756 (08.30am to 05.00pm Sri Lankan Time on Weekdays) or you also can send a message through the Amana Internet Banking System.

 

15. CAN I CHANGE MY PASSWORD OR TRANSACTION PIN?

You can change your passwords after you have logged on to the Internet Banking System. "Change Password" option is found in My Profile" menu. Both Password and Transaction PIN can be changed using this option.

16. WHAT HAPPENS IF MY USER ID GETS LOCKED?

User ID and the Password is your KEY to the Bank.

To protect against any unauthorized access to your accounts, your login is automatically disabled after 3 consecutive login failures. Thereafter the message "You have exceeded maximum unsuccessful Login attempts. - User ID Locked" appears whenever you try to login.

In case your access is locked, please contact Help Desk at Tel: +94 11 7756756 (08.30am to 05.00pm Sri Lankan Time on Weekdays) to re-activate your account.

17. WHAT IF I FORGET MY PASSWORD OR PIN?

You will have an option to use the Forget Password option in the Login page if you forget your password and then you will have to answer a few verification questions once it is cleared you can reset it to a new Password. If you cant remember your password verification details or if you forget your Transaction PIN, you are required to fill in a maintenance form from the nearest Amana Bank branch.

The Bank will then regenerate a new Password/PIN and send it to you in a new PIN Mailer to your registered correspondence address. The issue of the new Password Mailer is similar to issuing your original PIN mailer.
